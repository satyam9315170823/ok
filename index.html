About Us

Empowering Communities, Transforming Lives

Omsudha Kutumbkum Foundation is a non-profit organisation established on August 24, 2023, dedicated to empowering communities through healthcare, education, women’s empowerment, skill development, and environmental sustainability.

Mrs. Renu Sharma, Director

Registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act and recognised by MSME & NITI Aayog, we also hold 80G & 12A certifications and CSR registration (CSR00076445), enabling impactful projects and donor tax benefits. Our initiatives include free health camps, awareness drives, skill training, women empowerment programs, and environmental conservation activities.
